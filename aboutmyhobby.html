Let's get you interested

in today's world tabletop games create a huge variety of options to explore and play from. Let me give you a small tour on a couple of big categories.

  • Strategy games:
      • here you can play the game with tactical choices and trick which can win or loose you the game. It is usually played against an another player or players.
  • Simulation games:
      • Here you can play on a board with the game money you have and build up your own empire. just like in Monopoly.
  • Card games:
      • Here you have a bunch of cards with instructions or resources which will allow you to play the game according your cards.
  • Roleplaying games:
      • Here you are personating a playable character in a fantasy world alongside your friends who controlles other characters. In this game the end goal is created by the players and what they decide to do with their options. The last player is a so called "Dungeon Master" who's role is to tell you the story in which you are playing with your character.
  • There are other types but i don't want to keep you here for the next couple of hours. You could use that time to play play a game with your friend!
